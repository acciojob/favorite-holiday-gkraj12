Goa


Goa is a beautiful holiday destination known for its beaches, nightlife, and food.

  1. Baga Beach is famous for water sports.
  2. Fort Aguada offers a historic experience.
  3. Dudhsagar Falls is a must-visit natural wonder.
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesus is a popular church.
  5. Anjuna Beach is known for its vibe.
  • Goan Fish Curry is very popular.
  • Prawn Balchao is spicy and tasty.
  • Bebinca is a famous dessert.
  • Vindaloo is a must-try spicy dish.
  • Xacuti is loved for its rich flavor.
